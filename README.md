1.1 Brief History

Korabloom Herbal Haircare was established to address the growing demand for natural and herbal hair care solutions. The business focuses on helping individuals experiencing hair loss, thinning edges, weak hairlines, and scalp damage through plant-based products. Inspired by traditional herbal remedies and modern hair care practices, Korabloom developed natural formulations using ingredients such as rosemary, fenugreek, hibiscus, and nourishing oils to promote healthy hair growth and scalp wellness.

1.2 Mission Statement

"To provide safe, effective, and affordable herbal hair care solutions that promote healthy hair growth and restore confidence naturally."

1.3 Vision Statement

"To become South Africa's leading herbal hair care brand, recognized for delivering natural, innovative, and trusted solutions for healthy hair growth."

1.4 Target Audience

• Women aged 18–45 experiencing hair thinning and hair loss
• Individuals with natural, curly, coily, and textured hair
• Students, young professionals, and mothers
• Consumers seeking herbal and chemical-free products
• Social-media-active beauty and wellness enthusiasts
• Customers interested in organic and natural beauty solutions
